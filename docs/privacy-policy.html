Privacy Policy for AnkiFlow AI

Last updated: April 8, 2026

AnkiFlow AI ("the app") is developed and maintained by Amin Sajedian. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers. The developer has no access to any information entered into the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your Google Gemini API key locally on your device using Android's secure local storage (AsyncStorage). This key is never transmitted to our servers — it is sent directly from your device to Google's Gemini API only when you request AI-generated content.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API — used to generate AI content for your flashcards. Your prompts are sent directly to Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • AnkiDroid — communication is local only (on-device). No flashcard data leaves your device through AnkiFlow AI.

Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely to reach the Google Gemini API. No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.

Children's Privacy

AnkiFlow AI is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at aminsajedian@gmail.com.
